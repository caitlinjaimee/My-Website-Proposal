Fun Cakes About Me — Website Project Proposal

1. Website Name
•	 Name: Fun Cakes About Me

2. Brief History
Fun Cakes About Me is a fan site created in honor of the legendary TV show Cake Boss. As a fan, the goal is to promote and share the unique and creative cakes featured on the show. This fansite is designed for anyone who enjoys desserts and wants to explore some of the best, most imaginative cakes that can be customized for any event.

3. Mission
To celebrate Cake Boss as a fan while sharing the journey behind creating the perfect cake.

4. Vision
To inspire visitors to enjoy the show as much as they love cake and encourage engagement with the art of cake design.

5. Audience
•	Catering companies
•	Cake enthusiasts
•	Anyone interested in purchasing or learning about customized, creative cakes

6. Goals
•	Share and promote my favorite baking show (Cake Boss)
•	Encourage visitors to interact with the site via feedback
•	Maintain long-term engagement by showcasing new cake designs and interactive features

7. Features & Functionality
•	Home Page: Key information about the site and featured cakes
•	Gallery: Insider pictures, cake designs, and fun memes
•	About Page: Information about cake designs and inspiration
•	Build a Cake Page: Users can select cake size, layers, shape, colors, decorations, and messages
•	Rating / Feedback Page: Collect user feedback and ratings

8. Design & User Experience
•	Theme: blue
•	Focus: Informative yet interactive, allowing users to provide feedback and create their own cake designs
•	Style: calm, trendy
9. Hosting & Domain Name
•	Hosting and domain name: FunCakesAboutMe
•	Free or low-cost hosting solutions will be considered for fansite purposes

10. Budget
•	No budget required for this project


11. Timeline
•	Total Duration: 2 months (8 weeks)
o	Part 1: Weeks 1–3 — Website planning, homepage, and navbar/footer layout
o	Part 2: Weeks 4–5 — Gallery, About page, and Build a Cake page development
o	Part 3: Weeks 6–8 — Feedback page, testing, and final adjustments
•	Goal: Complete the project before the deadline
